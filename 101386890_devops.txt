******************DevOps******************

DevOps is a set of practices, tools, and a cultural philosophy that automate and integrate the processes between software development and IT teams.

DevOps team is the combination of development and operations team.
It helps the business to deliver applications quickly and efficiently.

Advantages of DevOps Adoption
	1. Reducing IT overhead
	2. Faster product delivery
	3. Enforce best security practices
	4. Remove manuial tasks
	5. Improved communication and collaboration
