SDLC - Software Development Life Cycle

Advantages of SDLC:
	- Provides standardized framework 
	- Aids project planning, estimating, and scheduling
	- Easier project tracking and control
	- Faster development
	- Decrease overall cost of production

Phase
	1. Planning
	2. Coding
	3. Building
	4. Testing
	5. Release
	6. Deploy
	7. Operate
	8. Monitor
